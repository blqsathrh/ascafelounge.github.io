About Our History and Background

A's Cafe was born out of the passion of 5 friends who wished to bring an aesthetic plus minimalist vibe cafe that can be a place for people to study and also spending their time. It has been inspired by their own experienced after go for travelling in others countries such as Paris, Turkey and Korea.

Inspired by the food while travel this 5 friend decided to open their cafe and set their mission.Their main mission is:

  • Give Malaysian taste of france with reasonable price.
  • Giving expats who live in malaysia their hometown taste.
  • pay less for good quality.

They returned to Malaysia and opened A's Café in 2018, in a bustling dining crowd, they are commencing a new chapter in Pulau Pinang which are at Seberang Jaya. Their cafe presented a most delicious and appetizing bun, cake and coffee as they can. For example, Prinsesstårta, a layer cake from Sweden that is covered in a hard topping of marzipan and also Pogácsa, a Hungarian small, bite sized pastry that can be filled with pastry cream.
